About Us

Welcome to my website, Magical Disney World Tips! I want to tell you about why I made this website. My family and I go to Disney World at least once or twice a year. Throughout all my years being in Disney I have learned a lot of helpful tips that I want to share. I have helped my friends and their parents with planning their trip because I know a lot about Disney. I wanted to share my information with everyone because I want people to have the best experience when they are on vacation. I hope my information on this page will help you with your next Disney World trip!
